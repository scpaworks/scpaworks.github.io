User Interface

Below is the interface for managing appointments in Apptoto.

How to Make an Appointment

  1. Click the Add Appt. button
  2. In the Appointment Editor, enter the information for your appointment.
  3. Click Save to save the appointment details. You will be prompted to enter participants.
  4. Enter any participants you want to add to the event.
  5. Review the event information, and hit close to finish editing.
